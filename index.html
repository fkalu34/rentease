Questions

Straight answers.

Is RentEase a loan or a bank?
No. RentEase is a rent payment coordination and flexible contribution management service. We help structure and facilitate rent payments between tenants and landlords — we are not a bank, lender, or financial institution. No deposit-taking activity is conducted.
How quickly can my landlord be paid?
Once your application is approved and your contribution plan is agreed, landlord payment typically happens within 3–5 business days. Urgent cases are handled faster.
What if my landlord refuses to cooperate?
Our team will reach out to your landlord directly to explain the process. Most landlords prefer this arrangement because they receive full payment immediately — better than waiting for a tenant to gather funds.
How does the inflation protection work?
Once your rent is paid, that figure is locked. If your landlord increases rent at renewal, that increase only applies to the next plan — not the current year you've already secured.
What happens if I miss a monthly contribution?
Life happens. We have a short grace window and will contact you to restructure if needed. Our goal is to keep you housed — not to create more stress for you.
I am a landlord. How does RentEase help me?
RentEase partners with landlords to provide verified tenants and guarantee consistent rent payments. We screen every tenant before placement and coordinate payments so you receive your rent on time without chasing anyone. Contact us to register your property.
What flexible payment options are available?
We offer monthly and quarterly contribution structures depending on your income pattern. During your application our team will discuss which option suits you best and build your plan accordingly.
