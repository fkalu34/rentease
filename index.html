Questions

Straight answers.

Is RentEase a loan or a bank?
No. RentEase is a rent payment coordination and flexible contribution management service. We help structure and facilitate rent payments between tenants and landlords — we are not a bank, lender, or financial institution. No deposit-taking activity is conducted.
How quickly can my landlord be paid?
Once your application is approved and your contribution plan is agreed, landlord payment typically happens within 3–5 business days. Urgent cases are handled faster.
What if my landlord refuses to cooperate?
Our team will reach out to your landlord directly to explain the process. Most landlords prefer this arrangement because they receive full payment immediately — better than waiting for a tenant to gather funds.
How does the inflation protection work?
Once your rent is paid, that figure is locked. If your landlord increases rent at renewal, that increase only applies to the next plan — not the current year you've already secured.
What happens if I miss a monthly contribution?
Life happens. We have a short grace window and will contact you to restructure if needed. Our goal is to keep you housed — not to create more stress for you.
I am a landlord. How does RentEase help me?
RentEase partners with landlords to provide verified tenants and guarantee consistent rent payments. We screen every tenant before placement and coordinate payments so you receive your rent on time without chasing anyone. Contact us to register your property.
What flexible payment options are available?
We offer monthly and quarterly contribution structures depending on your income pattern. During your application our team will discuss which option suits you best and build your plan accordingly.

Terms & Conditions

Last updated: May 2026

1. About RentEase

Rent Ease Services Limited (RC-9513604) is a rent payment coordination and contribution management service registered in Nigeria. We are not a bank, microfinance institution, or financial lender. We do not accept deposits or provide credit facilities.

2. Our Service

RentEase facilitates rent payment arrangements between tenants and landlords. We coordinate the payment of annual rent to landlords on behalf of tenants and manage structured contribution schedules for the recovery of the coordinated amount, plus a service coordination fee.

3. Service Coordination Fee

RentEase charges a service coordination fee calculated as 18% of the remaining rent balance after the customer's initial contribution. This fee is agreed and disclosed upfront before any commitment is made.

4. Initial Contribution

Before RentEase coordinates payment to your landlord, you are required to make an initial contribution of between 20% and 50% of your annual rent amount, as agreed during onboarding. This contribution is applied directly toward your rent and is non-refundable once the landlord has been paid.

5. Customer Obligations

By engaging RentEase you agree to: (a) provide accurate and truthful information during onboarding; (b) make contributions on agreed dates; (c) notify RentEase immediately of any change in circumstances that may affect your ability to contribute; (d) ensure your guarantor is aware of and consents to their role.

6. Default

If a contribution is not received within 7 days of the agreed date, a late fee will apply. Continued default may result in escalation to your guarantor, legal recovery action, and reporting to relevant authorities. RentEase will always attempt to reach a resolution before escalation.

7. Guarantor

Where a guarantor is required, they accept joint responsibility for outstanding contributions if the primary customer defaults. Guarantors must be Aba-based, independently verifiable, and must provide consent in writing.

8. Landlord Coordination

RentEase coordinates directly with your landlord. By engaging our service, you authorise RentEase to communicate with your landlord on your behalf for the purpose of rent payment.

9. Limitation of Liability

RentEase is not liable for delays caused by landlord unavailability, incomplete information provided by the customer, or circumstances beyond our reasonable control. Our liability is limited to the service coordination fee paid.

10. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Any disputes shall be resolved in a court of competent jurisdiction in Abia State.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Rent Ease Services Limited ("RentEase", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information during onboarding and service delivery: full name, phone number, email address, NIN (National Identification Number), home and work address, employment or business details, monthly income information, landlord contact details, and guarantor information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to: (a) verify your identity and assess your contribution capacity; (b) coordinate rent payment with your landlord; (c) manage your contribution schedule; (d) contact you regarding your account; (e) comply with our legal obligations under Nigerian law.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information with: your landlord (for the purpose of rent coordination), your guarantor (where relevant to default recovery), and legal or regulatory authorities where required by law.

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. Customer records are stored securely and access is restricted to authorised RentEase personnel only.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for the duration of your engagement with RentEase and for a period of 5 years after your final transaction, as required for legal and regulatory compliance. You may request deletion of your data after this period.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal information we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate information; request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements); withdraw consent for communications at any time.
